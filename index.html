Instructions

This is a deck of cards with funny, wierd, curious questions to help people know each other better

Take turns swiping the cards and answering the questions as a group or a pair. Or make up your own rules!

Each time you refresh the page, we pick a new set of 32 questions from a bank of 108, so each experience will hopefully stay fresh!

Enjoy and thanks for playing!
